Blood Donation Drive

About this Event

Join the HealthFirst Foundation for our annual Blood Donation Drive to make a difference in the lives of patients in need. Blood donations are crucial for surgeries, trauma care, and cancer treatments, and your contribution can save lives. The drive will take place in a comfortable, professional setting where medical staff will be available to ensure a safe and smooth donation experience. Every donor will receive a complimentary health screening, light refreshments, and a small token of appreciation. Come and be part of this life-saving initiative!
Event Highlights:
Free health screening for all donors
Refreshments and snacks provided post-donation
Certificate of appreciation for all participants
